Combat Robotics Series (25 / 15 / 8 KG)

CNC Chassis · High-Energy Weapons · National RoboWars Podiums

A family of three combat robots built for national RoboWars competitions: 25 kg, 15 kg, and 8 kg weight classes. Each robot features a CNC-machined chassis, tuned drivetrain, and carefully balanced weapon system, optimized for reliability under extreme impacts.

Series Overview

The robots were iteratively designed across multiple seasons, with each class taking lessons from the previous builds in terms of weapon integration, armor layout, and serviceability. The result is a consistent design language: compact frames, modular armor plates, and fast access to electronics.

All three robots were built end-to-end: CAD, manufacturing, assembly, wiring, and competition-day repair. The projects provided hands-on experience with impact-resistant design, rapid fault isolation, and tuning ESCs, motors, and batteries for short, high-power matches.

Individual Robots

  • 25 KG Vertical Spinner – 2nd place at NIT Trichy.
  • 15 KG Drum Spinner – 1st place at Anna University Chennai.
  • 8 KG Front-hinged Wedge – 2nd place at Amrita University Bangalore.
