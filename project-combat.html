Combat Robotics Series – 25 KG, 15 KG, 8 KG

I’ve built and iterated on three different combat robots in 25 KG, 15 KG, and 8 KG weight classes for national-level RoboWars events. Each robot is a complete mechanical, electrical, and reliability challenge – from energy transfer and armor to serviceability between matches.

Robots

  • 25 KG Combat Robot – 2nd place, NIT Trichy
  • 15 KG Combat Robot – 1st place, Anna University Chennai
  • 8 KG Combat Robot – 2nd place, Amrita Bangalore

All competitions are national-level technical festivals (event names can be updated later).
