Combat Robotics Series

Over multiple national competitions, I designed and built three combat robots across the 25 KG, 15 KG, and 8 KG categories. These robots competed at major National Tech Fests in India and secured podium finishes and prize money at NIT Trichy, Anna University Chennai, and Amrita University Bengaluru.

Robots

25 KG Combat Robot – 2nd Place, NIT Trichy (₹50,000) 15 KG Combat Robot – 1st Place, Anna University (₹40,000) 8 KG Combat Robot – 2nd Place, Amrita Bengaluru (₹25,000)

Skills Demonstrated

  • Mechanical design for impact resistance and energy transfer.
  • Power electronics, motor selection, and battery safety at high currents.
  • Rapid prototyping, machining, and assembly under competition deadlines.
  • Match strategy, driving skills, and between-match repair workflows.
