Combat Robotics Series – 25 / 15 / 8 KG

CNC Machining · Weapon Design · High-Energy Impacts

A family of combat robots across 25 kg, 15 kg, and 8 kg weight classes, each optimized for robustness, energy transfer, and serviceability. These robots have collectively earned multiple national podium finishes and significant prize money.

Robots in the Series

  • 25 KG Vertical Spinner – CNC billet chassis, high-moment vertical weapon.
  • 15 KG Drum / Bar Hybrid – agile drivetrain tuned for aggressive driving.
  • 8 KG Compact Weapon Bot – optimized for low-inertia, fast spin-up.

Across all designs, the focus is on repeatable hard hits, modular repair, and keeping the robot driveable even after sustaining damage.

Highlights

  • Multiple national tech fest podiums across India.
  • Extensive use of CNC machining, hardox/EN materials, and TIG welding.
  • Iterative upgrades after every competition based on damage analysis.
  • Experience coordinating fabrication, electronics, and pit operations.
