About Me

I am a robotics engineer with a strong foundation in ROS / ROS2, SLAM, navigation, obstacle avoidance, and real-time perception. I specialize in taking robotic systems from CAD and simulation all the way to physical deployment — integrating LiDAR, IMU, cameras, and robust control frameworks.

My experience spans autonomous 4-wheel robots, omni-directional kinematics, combat robotics, agricultural robots, and advanced localization systems. I enjoy building scalable, reliable systems backed by clean documentation and data-driven insights.

What I Do

Full robotics pipeline: CAD → simulation → ROS2 bring-up → SLAM → Navigation → obstacle avoidance → testing & field deployment.

How I Work

Iterative, experiment-driven, and reliability-focused — I break complex systems into modular components and validate through simulation + real-world testing.

What I’m Seeking
