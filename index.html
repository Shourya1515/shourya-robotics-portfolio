About Me

I am a robotics engineer with a strong foundation in ROS / ROS2, SLAM, navigation, and real-time perception. I enjoy taking ideas from CAD and simulation all the way to physical systems — integrating LiDAR, IMU, cameras, and control software into robust robots.

My experience spans autonomous 4-wheel robots, omni-directional kinematics, combat robotics, agricultural robots, and analytical reporting. I like combining hands-on building with clear documentation and data-driven insight.
