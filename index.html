About Me

I am a robotics engineer with strong experience in ROS2, SLAM, autonomous navigation, kinematics, and real-time perception. I specialize in bringing up robots from scratch— modeling them in simulation, integrating LiDAR/IMU/cameras, and deploying intelligent navigation pipelines.

My projects span autonomous 4-wheel robots, omni-directional platforms, combat robots, agricultural systems, and sensor-fusion pipelines. I enjoy solving challenging robotics problems by combining engineering intuition with clean system design.
