8 KG Combat Robot – 2nd Place (Amrita University Bangalore)

This 8 KG combat robot achieved 2nd place and earned a Twenty Five Thousand Rupees cash prize at the National Tech Fest conducted by Amrita University Bengaluru. The robot focused on lightweight durability, fast maneuverability, and resilience under repeated impacts.

Project Overview

Engineered with a lightweight frame and a speed-optimized drivetrain, this robot excelled in mobility and controlled aggression. Its compact geometry made it difficult for opponents to land damaging hits, while its drivetrain allowed fast repositioning in the arena.

Highlights

  • 2nd Place – Amrita University National Tech Fest
  • Ultra-lightweight design with high maneuverability
  • Fast acceleration and quick directional control
  • Shock-resistant internal mounting system
