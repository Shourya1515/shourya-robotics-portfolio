8 KG Combat Robot – Wedge Bot

2nd Place · ₹25,000 Prize · Amrita University Bangalore National Tech Fest

A compact 8 kg robot focused on control, low profile, and reliability, acting as a testbed for new drivetrain and armor ideas.

Gallery

Project Overview

The 8 kg bot trades raw weapon power for aggressive driving and survivability. It is built around a low wedge that can get underneath opponents and push them into arena hazards or out of the engagement zone.

The small size made it an ideal platform to quickly iterate on armor layouts, wheel protection, and internal packaging before scaling the ideas to heavier classes.

Competition Result

The robot earned 2nd place and a prize of ₹25,000 at the national-level tech fest hosted by Amrita University Bangalore.

Technical Details

  • Lightweight wedge chassis with reinforced front lip.
  • High-torque drive for shoving heavier opponents.
  • Electronics packaged for quick access and replacement.
  • Designed to be easy to transport and field.

Learnings

This robot reinforced the importance of driver skill, weight distribution, and ground clearance management, especially in lighter classes where every gram matters.
