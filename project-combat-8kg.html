8 KG Combat Robot – Compact Weapon Bot

2nd Place · ₹25,000 Prize · National Tech Fest, Amrita University Bangalore

A compact 8 kg combat robot focused on fast spin-up, low inertia, and efficient energy transfer—ideal for smaller arenas and dense fight schedules.

Gallery

Design Highlights

  • Compact footprint for high agility and quick directional changes.
  • Efficient weapon system designed for rapid spin-up.
  • Optimized weight distribution to stay planted during hits.

Competition Outcome

At the national tech fest hosted by Amrita University, Bangalore, the 8 kg robot earned 2nd place and a prize of ₹25,000. The robot consistently delivered clean impacts and remained functional across multiple intense matches.

Lessons from this lighter-weight platform informed the control tuning and weapon behaviour of the larger 15 kg and 25 kg robots.
