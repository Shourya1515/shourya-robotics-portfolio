8 KG Combat Robot – National Runner-Up

This agile 8 KG combat robot won 2nd place at the National Tech Fest conducted by Amrita University Bengaluru, earning a cash prize of ₹25,000.

Designed for speed, maneuverability, and rapid attack cycles, the robot excelled in a lightweight category where precision driving and quick directional changes are critical to victory.

Gallery

Project Overview

This robot was optimized for the lightweight category, focusing heavily on speed-to-weight ratio and structural resilience. Its compact frame and centered mass distribution enabled sharp turns, evasive maneuvers, and stable weapon engagement.

Reinforced wheel guards and precise electronic packaging ensured uninterrupted performance during the fast-paced battles typical of the 8 KG class.

Technical Breakdown

  • Lightweight aluminum chassis optimized for maneuverability
  • High-speed brushless drive motors
  • Low-profile design for strategic pushing and control
  • Electronics protected within shock-dampened mounts
  • High-traction wheels for tight turning radius
  • Optimized for endurance during sustained combat rounds

Outcome

The robot’s agility and precision were key to its success, outperforming multiple teams to secure the Runner-Up position. This project showcases strong skills in lightweight chassis design, rapid control response, and combat strategy.
