8 KG Combat Robot – 2nd Place (Amrita University Bengaluru)

Lightweight, high-mobility combat robot designed for speed and evasive driving. This robot earned 2nd place and a ₹25,000 cash prize at the National Tech Fest conducted by Amrita University Bengaluru.

Gallery

Project Overview

In the 8 KG category, every gram matters. The robot’s chassis and armor were optimized for weight while still providing enough rigidity to survive repeated impacts. The strategy focused on outmaneuvering heavier opponents, exploiting their spin-up time, and attacking from safe angles.

Technical Breakdown

  • Lightweight frame design prioritized power-to-weight ratio.
  • High-speed drive motors tuned for quick acceleration and directional changes.
  • Compact electronics packaging for minimized moment of inertia.
  • Traction-optimized wheels chosen for fast control on arena floor.
  • Electrical design with current protection and thermal awareness.

Outcome

Despite the constraints of the lighter weight class, the robot demonstrated excellent driving dynamics and survivability, reinforcing my ability to balance agility, structural design, and electrical robustness in constrained design spaces.
