15 KG Combat Robot – 1st Place (Anna University Chennai)

This 15 KG combat robot secured 1st place and won a Forty Thousand Rupees cash prize at the National Tech Fest conducted by Anna University Chennai. Designed for durability and pushing dominance, the robot features a CNC-machined chassis, high-torque drivetrain, and a precision wedge geometry.

Project Overview

The 15 KG bot is built around a rigid 6061-T6 aluminum frame optimized for energy absorption. Its low ground clearance wedge destabilizes opponents, while the high-current brushless motors deliver exceptional pushing force and acceleration. Throughout competition, the bot showcased consistent control, torque-heavy maneuvering, and superior endurance against heavy-hitting weapons.

Highlights

  • Winner – 1st Place at Anna University National Tech Fest
  • CNC-machined aluminum chassis optimized for rigidity
  • High torque BLDC drive enabling powerful shoves
  • Low profile wedge designed for aggressive combat
  • Modular internal layout for quick battlefield repair
