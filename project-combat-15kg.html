15 KG Combat Robot – Drum Spinner

1st Place · ₹40,000 Prize · Anna University Chennai National Tech Fest

A 15 kg drum-spinner robot optimized for reliability and bite, designed to deliver repeated hits without self-damage.

Gallery

Project Overview

The 15 kg robot was built to be a “workhorse” design: simple to drive, easy to service, and capable of taking and delivering hard hits. The drum weapon is carefully balanced, and the drivetrain is chosen to prioritize control over top speed.

Compared to the 25 kg bot, this design emphasizes protected electronics, modular side armor, and quick belt and chain replacement between matches.

Competition Result

The robot secured 1st place and a prize of ₹40,000 at the national-level tech fest conducted by Anna University Chennai.

Technical Details

  • Drum spinner weapon with hardened impactors.
  • Brushless drive system with mixing for smooth turns.
  • 4-wheel drive for better traction and pushing ability.
  • Electronics bay isolated from direct weapon hits.

Learnings

This bot highlighted the importance of drivetrain tuning and driver practice. A controllable, predictable robot often outperforms faster but less reliable designs.
