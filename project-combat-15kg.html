15 KG Combat Robot – Championship Winner

1st Place · ₹40,000 Prize · National Tech Fest, Anna University Chennai

A fast, aggressive 15 kg combat robot designed around a drum/bar-style weapon and highly responsive drivetrain, tuned for controlled rushes and precision driving in tight arenas.

Gallery

Design Focus

  • Balanced weapon to minimize self-damage and maintain driveability.
  • Robust wheel guards and armour to handle repeated side impacts.
  • Compact electronics layout for faster repairs between matches.

Competition Outcome

At the national tech fest conducted by Anna University, Chennai, this robot secured 1st place and won ₹40,000. Consistent control and reliability allowed the team to capitalise on opponent mistakes and finish fights either by knockout or judges' decision.

The success of this bot validated the drivetrain and weapon architecture and heavily influenced the design decisions for the other weight classes in the combat series.
