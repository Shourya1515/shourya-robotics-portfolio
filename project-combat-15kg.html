15 KG Combat Robot – National Tech Fest Champion

This 15 KG combat robot secured a 1st Place victory at the National Level Tech Fest conducted by Anna University, Chennai, earning a cash prize of ₹40,000.

Designed for high maneuverability and impact resistance, this robot features a reinforced metal chassis, precision weapon mount, and powerful drive motors optimized for fast directional control and aggressive combat.

Gallery

Project Overview

Built using a mix of aircraft-grade aluminum and steel reinforcements, this robot was engineered to withstand continuous high-energy hits. Its compact geometry enabled rapid turning and evasive maneuvers, allowing strategic control in the arena.

The design focused on achieving a balance between speed, armor, and weapon stability, resulting in a highly competitive configuration for the 15 KG category.

Technical Breakdown

  • High-mobility 4-wheel or 2-wheel drive configuration
  • Reinforced chassis for impact absorption
  • Brushless drive motors for quick acceleration
  • Polycarbonate armor shielding vulnerable components
  • Modular weapon mount suitable for wedge or drum systems
  • Internal compartment layout optimized for cooling and wiring safety

Outcome

The robot demonstrated exceptional combat performance — fast, stable, and durable throughout the competition. Its decisive victories led to securing the 1st Place Title at Anna University’s national event.
