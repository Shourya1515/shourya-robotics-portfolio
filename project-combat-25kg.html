25 KG Combat Robot – Vertical Spinner

2nd Place · ₹50,000 Prize · NIT Trichy National Tech Fest

A 25 kg vertical-spinner combat robot with a fully CNC-machined chassis, hardened weapon disk, and high-traction drive, built for national-level RoboWars at NIT Trichy.

Gallery

Project Overview

The 25 kg bot was designed around a compact, rigid frame to survive weapon-to-weapon engagements. The chassis was CNC-milled from aluminum billets, with pockets for weight reduction and stiffening ribs in high-load areas.

A brushless motor drives a vertical spinner weapon, capable of delivering high tip-speed impacts. The wedge and front forks are designed to get under opponents and feed them into the weapon.

Competition Result

The robot won 2nd place and a cash prize of ₹50,000 at the national-level tech fest hosted by National Institute of Technology Trichy.

Technical Details

  • CNC-machined aluminum chassis with bolt-on armor plates.
  • High-KV brushless weapon motor with belt drive to the vertical disk.
  • Brushless drive with high-traction wheels for powerful pushes.
  • Service-friendly layout for rapid weapon and wheel replacement.

Learnings

This build sharpened my understanding of impact-resistant design, stress concentration around weapon shafts, and the importance of quick repair workflows during tournaments.
