25 KG Combat Robot – Vertical Spinner

2nd Place · ₹50,000 Prize · National Tech Fest, NIT Trichy

A 25 kg vertical-spinner combat robot with a CNC-machined billet chassis, large-diameter weapon, and robust two-wheel drive. Designed to deliver high energy impacts while surviving full-contact fights in national-level arenas.

Gallery

Design & Construction

  • CNC-machined billet aluminium chassis for stiffness and serviceability.
  • High-moment vertical spinner weapon driven by a powerful motor.
  • Two-wheel drive with grippy tyres for reliable pushing and control.
  • Layered armor strategy around key electronics and battery pack.

Competition Performance

At the national tech fest hosted by NIT Trichy, the robot secured 2nd place and won ₹50,000 in prize money. The design delivered heavy hits while staying driveable even after taking damage, which was critical in back-to-back fights.

Post-event teardown informed improvements to weapon balancing, wheel hub design, and shock isolation for electronics, all of which fed into later iterations of the combat series.
