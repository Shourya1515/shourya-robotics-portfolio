25 KG Combat Robot – 2nd Place (NIT Trichy)

Heavyweight combat robot engineered for high-energy impacts and durability. This robot secured 2nd place and a ₹50,000 cash prize at the National Tech Fest conducted by NIT Trichy.

Gallery

Project Overview

The 25 KG robot was built around a CNC-machined 6061-T6 aluminum chassis designed to absorb and redirect impact forces from high-RPM spinning weapons. Its architecture focused on structural rigidity, protected powertrain layout, and fast serviceability between matches.

Technical Breakdown

  • Heavy-duty 6061-T6 aluminum chassis with stiffening ribs and pocketing.
  • High-inertia spinning weapon designed for energy transfer and bite.
  • Brushless drive system dimensioned for torque and acceleration under load.
  • High-discharge LiPo battery pack with fuse and isolation for safety.
  • Armored side walls and top plate to protect electronics and batteries.
  • Modular assembly for rapid motor, wheel, and weapon maintenance.

Outcome

The robot consistently survived full-contact fights against other heavy spinners while maintaining drivability and weapon reliability, demonstrating my experience in high-load mechanical design, power electronics, and competition strategy.
