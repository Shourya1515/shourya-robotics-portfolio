25 KG Combat Robot – 2nd Place (NIT Trichy)

This 25 KG combat robot secured 2nd place and won a Fifty Thousand Rupees cash prize at the National Tech Fest conducted by National Institute of Technology (NIT) Trichy. Built for maximum power and structural durability, this robot featured a CNC-machined heavy-duty chassis, reinforced armor, and a high-inertia spinning weapon system.

Project Overview

This robot was engineered for high-energy combat scenarios. Its robust frame and carefully balanced center of gravity allowed it to survive direct hits from high-RPM spinners. The drivetrain and weapon assembly were tuned for torque delivery and rapid engagement, and the robot consistently demonstrated reliability throughout the event.

Highlights

  • 2nd Place – NIT Trichy National Tech Fest
  • ₹50,000 prize for overall performance
  • CNC-machined 6061-T6 aluminum body with impact-resistant geometry
  • High-inertia spinning drum weapon for maximum damage
  • Reinforced internal mounts and vibration isolation
