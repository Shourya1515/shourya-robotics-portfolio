25 KG Combat Robot – National Tech Fest (NIT Trichy)

This 25 KG combat robot was engineered for high-intensity arena battles and competed in the National Level Tech Fest at NIT Trichy, securing an outstanding 2nd Place finish along with a cash prize of ₹50,000.

The robot featured a precision-machined aluminum chassis, a powerful vertical spinning drum weapon, and highly optimized armor designed for impact resistance, stability, and durability in full-contact competitions.

Gallery

Project Overview

The 25 KG robot was built from a combination of CNC-machined 6061 aluminum, custom weapon mounts, high-torque BLDC drive motors, and precision bearings. The weapon system delivered devastating impacts while maintaining rotational balance and efficiency.

The design also focused on ease of maintenance, quick part replacement, and effective defensive geometry, making it well-suited for the demanding nature of national competition battles.

Technical Breakdown

  • 6061-T6 Aluminum CNC-machined chassis and removable armor plates
  • High-speed vertical spinning drum weapon
  • Independent dual drive motors for maneuverability
  • Polycarbonate top shield and reinforced wheel guards
  • LiPo battery system with ESC thermal protection
  • Optimized for durability, reliability, and high-energy impacts

Outcome

The robot excelled in multiple rounds of the national competition, demonstrating exceptional durability, weapon performance, and driving control. Its 2nd place victory at NIT Trichy stands as a major highlight in my combat robotics experience.
